Description:

FastQC is one of the preferred tools for quality control. This tool analyses FASTQ files, and produces graphs to allow fast inspection of sequencing data sets.

Cutadapt is one of the first tools to perform trimming and filtering of NGS data. This tool has been widely adopted, and has also been used as a baseline for other data preprocessing tools, such as Sickle and Trim Galore!.
