Description:

FastQC is one of the preferred tools for quality control. This tool analyses FASTQ files, and produces graphs to allow fast inspection of sequencing data sets.

With its high speed, sensitivity, and customizable settings, PRINSEQ enables users to perform filtering and trimming of FASTQ files. This tool also provides a graphical overview of the quality of the provided input data sets. Thus, like FastQC, PRINSEQ can also be used to assess the quality of FASTQ files. Unfortunately PRINSEQ is not able to handle multiple threads. Available parallelized versions of PRINSEQ are third-party implementations and thus not available in Galaxy.
