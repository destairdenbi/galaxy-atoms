Description:

With its high speed, sensitivity, and customizable settings, PRINSEQ enables users to perform filtering and trimming of FASTQ files. This tool also provides a graphical overview of the quality of the provided input data sets.
PRINSEQ is not able to handle multiple threads. Available parallelized versions of PRINSEQ are third-party implementations and thus not available in Galaxy.
