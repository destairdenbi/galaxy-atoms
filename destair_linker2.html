BS/RRBS-Seq analysis

In this analysis we will build a BS/RRBS-Seq workflow to perform the quantification of methylated cytosines (m5C) in human placenta, given 4 control and 4 treatment data sets of paired-end Illumina sequences.
The datasets have been downsampled for demonstration purposes.
This case-study follows the investigation conducted by Lim et al, 2017.
